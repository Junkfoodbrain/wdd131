Site Plan Document

Site Name

  • TheApricotCottage.com

Why did you choose this name

  • Because it is the name of an actual home business that sells sourdough
  • Because it says "this is sourdough" and it also says this is a "cottage" bakery. It gives the person who sees the site an idea of what kind of business we are.

Site Purpose

  • To sell homemade sourdough
  • To inform potential customers of the benefits of sourdough
  • To provide tips and tricks to sourdough baking at home

Scenarios

  • How do I purchase your sourdough products?
  • How can I contact the baker for a special order?
  • How can I subscribe to a newsletter or updates?
  • How can I contact the baker for feedback or questions?

Color Scheme

  • Primary: #b89c79 (camel)
  • Secondary: #607b79 (deep teal)
  • Accent: #ef7e09 (tiger orange)
  • Accent2: #f7cc61 (tuscan sun)

Typography

  • Headings Font: Simonetta
  • Body Font: Zain
